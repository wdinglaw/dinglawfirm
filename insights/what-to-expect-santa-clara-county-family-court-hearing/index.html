What to Expect at a Santa Clara County Family Court Hearing

A family court hearing is not a conversation in the hallway. It is a structured legal proceeding where preparation, concise presentation, and relevant evidence matter.

By Law Offices of Wenyu Ding. This article is general information, not legal advice.

Know what issue is actually before the court

A hearing may be limited to custody, support, temporary orders, restraining orders, or enforcement. Preparation should match the specific request.

Frequently asked questions

Will I have to speak?

Possibly. Some hearings involve attorney argument only, while others may include questions or testimony.

How early should I prepare?

Preparation should begin well before the hearing date so documents, declarations, and strategy are organized.

Can hearings be continued?

Sometimes, but continuances are not automatic and depend on the circumstances.
