How RSUs Are Treated in California Divorce

RSUs are common in Silicon Valley divorce cases. They may affect property division, support, cash flow, and settlement timing, so they should be analyzed early and with care.

By Law Offices of Wenyu Ding. This article is general information, not legal advice.

RSUs can have mixed character

Depending on the grant date, vest date, employment purpose, and marriage timeline, RSUs may include community and separate property components.

Frequently asked questions

Are all RSUs divided equally?

Not necessarily. Characterization depends on the facts and applicable analysis.

Do unvested RSUs count?

Unvested RSUs may be relevant, but treatment depends on the purpose and timing of the grant.

Can RSUs affect monthly support?

Yes. Equity compensation may be considered in support analysis depending on the circumstances.
