High-Asset Divorce Silicon Valley

High-Asset Divorce Representation for Silicon Valley Professionals

Complex estates require integrated legal and financial planning. Our approach addresses RSUs and options, startup equity, business interests, retirement accounts and QDRO considerations, real estate portfolios, and high-income support issues through a single structured strategy.

Equity Compensation

RSUs, options, vesting schedules, and tax-sensitive allocation strategies require precise treatment in both property and support analysis.

Business and Investment Assets

Closely held interests and investment portfolios can involve valuation timing, characterization disputes, and long-term liquidity planning.

Support and Cash-Flow Structure

High-income cases often involve variable compensation patterns and require disciplined modeling for temporary and long-term support positions.

Why Strategy Matters

In high-asset matters, isolated decisions can create downstream consequences across support, tax exposure, and enforceability. Strategy must be coordinated from the outset, with issue sequencing that preserves leverage and reduces avoidable dispute points.

Our representation is intentionally measured: complete records, clear risk framing, and practical pathways toward resolution.
